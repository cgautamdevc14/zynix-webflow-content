Zynix AI SMS Program

Zynix AI sends SMS messages to patients and users for customer care and account-related purposes, including appointment reminders, care coordination updates, scheduling notifications, and account service alerts.

Message Frequency

Message frequency may vary based on your activity, but you can expect up to 8 messages per month. You will never receive more than 4 messages in a single day.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Opt-In

You can opt in to receive SMS messages from Zynix AI by:

  • Submitting the SMS consent form at https://www.zynix.ai/sms-consent
  • Checking the SMS opt-in checkbox on forms such as the contact / demo request page at https://www.zynix.ai/contact

Opt-Out

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, you will receive a confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

Help

For assistance, reply HELP to any message or contact us at:

  • Email: info@zynix.ai
  • Phone: (727) 261-1297
  • Website: https://www.zynix.ai/contact

Sample Messages

Zynix AI: Reminder – you have an appointment scheduled for tomorrow at 10 AM. Reply STOP to opt out.
Zynix AI: Your care team has an update regarding your recent visit. Reply STOP to opt out.
Zynix AI: You’re subscribed to appointment reminders & care updates. Msg frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help. Reply STOP to opt out.

Privacy

Your information will be handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy: https://www.zynix.ai/privacy-policy

Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchase.
