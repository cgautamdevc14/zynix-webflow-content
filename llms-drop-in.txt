> Zynix AI (Zynix Inc) builds the AI Operating System for Value-Based Care. Purpose-built for ACOs, health systems, health plans, FQHCs, and physician practices, Zynix deploys AI agents that execute care plans autonomously — not just recommend actions. The platform integrates clinical, claims, and operational data to power real-time analytics and intelligent automation across the care continuum. ## Platform

- [Zynix Platform](https://www.zynix.ai/platform): The unified AI Operating System for Value-Based Care — a single platform connecting data, analytics, AI agents, and care plans.
- [AI Agents](https://www.zynix.ai/agents): 12 specialized AI agents that take autonomous action across the care continuum — triage, scheduling, documentation, prior auth, post-discharge follow-up, and gap closure.
- [ZynScribe](https://www.zynix.ai/zynscribe): Ambient AI clinical documentation that captures and structures patient encounters in real time. 70% reduction in physician documentation time.
- [Care Plans](https://www.zynix.ai/care-plans): AI-generated executable care plans for TCM, CCM, AWV, HEDIS, and HCC closure — not static PDFs, but living plans that AI agents act on.
- [Zynix Data Analytics](https://www.zynix.ai/zynix-data-analytics): Real-time healthcare intelligence dashboards providing visibility into quality metrics, financial performance, and population health.
- [ZynixLLM](https://www.zynix.ai/company-zynixllm): Purpose-built healthcare language model with lower hallucination rates than general AI — trained for clinical and administrative healthcare workflows.
- [Predictive Activation Agents](https://www.zynix.ai/predictive-activation): Reach high-risk patients flagged by risk scores — converting predictions into outreach, appointments, and closed gaps.
- [EHR Integrations](https://www.zynix.ai/integrations): Native FHIR R4 and HL7 v2 integration with Epic, athenahealth, Cerner/Oracle Health, and eClinicalWorks. Real-time ADT, claims, labs, and pharmacy data.

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Zynix AI HIPAA compliant?
A: Yes. Zynix AI is fully HIPAA compliant and SOC 2 Type II certified. BAAs are available for all customers.

Q: How does Zynix AI integrate with Epic?
A: Via native FHIR R4 API and HL7 v2 feeds. Most Epic integrations go live within 2–4 weeks.

Q: How long does deployment take?
A: Typically 2–6 weeks for initial go-live. AI agents can be activated within days of data connection.

Q: What is the pricing?
A: Per-member-per-month (PMPM) or per-patient pricing. Contact info@zynix.ai for a custom quote.

Q: Does Zynix AI replace existing care management staff?
A: No. Zynix AI augments care management teams — handling outreach, documentation, and scheduling so clinicians can focus on complex cases and human-centered care.

Q: Is Zyncare the same as Zynix AI?
A: Zyncare is the patient-facing SMS brand used for outbound Twilio campaigns. Zynix AI / Zynix Inc is the platform and legal entity.
